About Vistone

Vistone is an AI-powered personal color analysis tool designed to help you discover the colors that love you back.

Finding the right colors to wear has traditionally been an expensive, manual process done by professional stylists. We've automated this process using advanced facial recognition and computer vision.

How It Works

When you upload a selfie, Vistone uses artificial intelligence to map your facial landmarks. It carefully extracts precise pixels from your cheeks, forehead, and nose while avoiding shadows, highlights, and facial hair.

These pure skin samples are then analyzed against the Google Monk Skin Tone Scale (a comprehensive 10-shade scale designed to represent a wider, more inclusive range of human skin tones). We map your skin to the closest Monk tone and calculate your underlying warmth or coolness to determine your undertone.

Professional Color Harmony

Based on your unique combination of Monk tone and undertone, Vistone references a strictly curated, professional Seasonal Color Analysis (SCA) database.

  • Best Colors: Specifically harmonized fabric shades that compliment your lightness and chroma, bringing out a healthy glow.
  • Colors to Avoid: Shades that may clash with your undertone, wash you out, or create an unflattering contrast.
